# Creative Content Generation & Analysis Lab

## Overview
Explore AI-powered content creation and analysis — from prompt engineering
to readability scoring, sentiment analysis, creative brainstorming, and
ethical content practices.

## AI Tutor
**Ada Byron** — Visionary at the intersection of science and art, guiding
you through the creative possibilities of computational text.

## Books & Topics

| Book | Topics |
|------|--------|
| Content Generation | Prompt patterns, article generation, story generation |
| Content Analysis | Readability metrics, sentiment analysis, style fingerprinting |
