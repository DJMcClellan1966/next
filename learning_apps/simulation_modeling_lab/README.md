# Advanced Simulation & Modeling Lab

## Overview
Explore the world of computational simulation — Monte Carlo methods,
dynamical systems, chaos theory, agent-based models, evolutionary
computation, and quantum-inspired optimization.

## AI Tutor
**Dr. Stanislaw Ulam** — Pioneer of Monte Carlo methods and computational
simulation, guiding you through the art of modeling complex systems.

## Books & Topics

| Book | Topics |
|------|--------|
| Monte Carlo & Stochastic Methods | π estimation, MC integration, MCMC, rare events |
| Dynamical Systems & Chaos | Logistic map, Lorenz attractor, predator-prey, bifurcation |
| Agent-Based & Evolutionary | Game of Life, flocking, genetic algorithms, co-evolution |
| Quantum-Inspired & Advanced | Simulated annealing, quantum walks, multiverse sim, quantum optimization |

## Running Standalone
```bash
cd learning_apps/simulation_modeling_lab
python app.py          # http://localhost:5018
```
